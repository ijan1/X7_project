About Us

Discover more about who we are and why we chose to build a donations website

Who are we?

We are a group of students from the Univeristy of Manchester willing to help people like us. In October 2020, we started working together on our first website. If you would like to help our website flourish, you can either donate something or get a donation. To do this, you will need to sign up using your university e-mail address.

Sign Up

Donate or get?

Whether you want to donate something or you are in need of something, we are here to help you. Our website is dedicated to students of The University of Manchester. Created during the pandemic, "Pass it on" is an attempt at re-connecting people, enforcing a sense of empathy among those who have been affected by the effects of Covid-19.
